NO MONEY.
NO HAPPY.

This is not a joke.
This is life.

You are not late.
You are just poor.

People with money buy early.
People without money buy later.
Early buyers get rich.
Late buyers pay the tax.
This time,
the tax goes back to the poor.

The less you have,
the more you get back.

NO MONEY. NO HAPPY.

We are still here.
